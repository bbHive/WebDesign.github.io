Our Story


Nifty was founded in 2015 with the goal to make fashion affordable. We started with nothing but passion for sustainable shopping and began selling secondhand items with their own stories.

While we are still a small store, we have become a hidden gem for thrift lovers online. Every item on hand was carefully selected to ensure that each customer finds something special with the utmost quality.
